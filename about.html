--- title: About ---

Hi, my name is David and I create interactive 3D web graphics.

As a front end web developer, design engineer and 3D artist with over 10 years of experience, I collaborate with cross-disciplinary teams to translate 2D concept art into real-time 3D models and animations, and translate static UX designs into clean, functional, responsive and accessible HTML, SCSS and Javascript.

My current favorite tools include React, Gatsby, Storybook, VS Code, Three.js, Blender and ZBrush, but I'm always learning, experimenting and exploring new ways to combine my creative and technical skills.
